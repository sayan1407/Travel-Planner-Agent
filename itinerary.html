Europe Trip Itinerary

Departing from Kolkata • May 28, 2026 to June 6, 2026 • 10 days

Day 1
May 28 • Kolkata to Paris
Fly to Paris

Overnight flight and check in. Evening stroll around the Seine.

Day 2
May 29 • Paris
Classic Paris

Eiffel Tower, Louvre exterior, and river cruise.

Day 3
May 30 • Paris to Amsterdam
Travel to Amsterdam

Take a morning train or flight and explore the canal district.

Day 4
May 31 • Amsterdam
Canals and museums

Anne Frank House area, canal cruise, and local cafes.

Day 5
June 1 • Amsterdam to Rome
Head to Rome

Fly to Italy and enjoy a relaxed evening in Trastevere.

Day 6
June 2 • Rome
Ancient Rome

Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill.

Day 7
June 3 • Rome to Florence
Florence day trip or overnight

Art, architecture, and Tuscan atmosphere.

Day 8
June 4 • Florence to Venice
Visit Venice

Grand Canal, St. Mark’s Square, and evening gondola ride.

Day 9
June 5 • Venice to Switzerland
Scenic alpine stop

Choose Zurich or Lucerne for mountain views and lake walks.

Day 10
June 6 • Return to Kolkata
Fly home

Transfer to the airport and return to Kolkata.

Tip: This is a flexible Europe sample plan. I can also turn it into a budget, luxury, or family-friendly version.
