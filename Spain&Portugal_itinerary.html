Spain & Portugal Trip

From Kolkata

Start: 2026-05-28 | Duration: 7 days

Day 1 — May 28: Kolkata to Barcelona

Fly overnight and arrive in Barcelona. Evening easy walk on Las Ramblas and dinner in the Gothic Quarter.

Day 2 — May 29: Barcelona

Sagrada Família, Park Güell, and waterfront sunset at Barceloneta.

Day 3 — May 30: Barcelona to Madrid

Train to Madrid. Explore Puerta del Sol, Plaza Mayor, and tapas bars.

Day 4 — May 31: Madrid to Lisbon

Fly to Lisbon. Ride Tram 28, visit Alfama, and enjoy Fado at night.

Day 5 — June 1: Lisbon

Belém Tower, Jerónimos Monastery, and pastel de nata tasting.

Day 6 — June 2: Lisbon to Porto

Train to Porto. Stroll Ribeira and cross Dom Luís I Bridge.

Day 7 — June 3: Porto and Return

Port wine cellars, riverside lunch, and departure planning for Kolkata.
