UK Adventure from Kolkata

28 May 2026 – 3 June 2026 • 7 Days

Trip Overview

A balanced first-time UK itinerary covering London’s icons, royal landmarks, Oxford, Bath, Stonehenge and Edinburgh. Start with an overnight flight from Kolkata and use fast trains within the UK.

LondonOxfordBathStonehengeEdinburgh
Day 1 • Thu, 28 May

Kolkata to London

Depart Kolkata for London, ideally via Doha, Dubai, Abu Dhabi or Delhi. Keep documents, visa, insurance and hotel confirmations handy.

Day 2 • Fri, 29 May

Classic London

Arrive in London, check in and take an easy walk through Westminster, Big Ben, Westminster Abbey, Trafalgar Square and the South Bank.

Day 3 • Sat, 30 May

Royal London & Museums

Visit Buckingham Palace, St James’s Park, the British Museum and Covent Garden. Evening option: West End show or Thames riverside dinner.

Day 4 • Sun, 31 May

Tower Bridge & Greenwich

Explore the Tower of London, Tower Bridge and Borough Market. Continue by river boat or train to Greenwich for the Royal Observatory area.

Day 5 • Mon, 1 Jun

Oxford Day Trip

Take a train to Oxford. Visit college courtyards, Bodleian Library area, Radcliffe Camera and enjoy a relaxed café lunch before returning to London.

Day 6 • Tue, 2 Jun

Bath & Stonehenge

Join a guided day tour or combine train and coach travel to see Stonehenge and Bath’s Roman Baths, Georgian streets and Pulteney Bridge.

Day 7 • Wed, 3 Jun

Edinburgh Highlights

Take an early train or short flight to Edinburgh. Visit Edinburgh Castle, Royal Mile and Calton Hill. Fly onward or return to London depending on tickets.

Practical Notes

  • Apply for a UK visitor visa well in advance from India.
  • Book Kolkata–London flights and UK trains early for better fares.
  • Carry a light jacket: late May and early June can still be cool and rainy.
  • Suggested bases: 5 nights London, 1 night Edinburgh or extend if possible.
