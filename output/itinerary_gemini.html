Spain & Portugal Grand Tour

From Kolkata to the Iberian Peninsula - 7 Days of Culture and Beauty

Departure: 2026-05-28 | Duration: 7 Days

Day 1: Thursday, May 28, 2026

Location: Travel Day (Kolkata to Lisbon, Portugal)

Activities:

  • Depart from Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport (CCU), Kolkata.
  • Enjoy your journey to Europe.
  • Arrive at Lisbon Portela Airport (LIS), Portugal.
  • Transfer to your hotel and check-in.
  • Relax and prepare for an exciting trip ahead.

Day 2: Friday, May 29, 2026

Location: Lisbon, Portugal

Activities:

  • Morning exploration of the historic Alfama district, including São Jorge Castle for panoramic city views.
  • Wander through the narrow, winding streets of Alfama.
  • Afternoon visit to Baixa and Chiado districts, Rossio Square, and a ride on the iconic Santa Justa Lift.
  • Evening dedicated to experiencing traditional Fado music in Alfama or Bairro Alto.

Day 3: Saturday, May 30, 2026

Location: Sintra, Portugal & Travel to Seville, Spain

Activities:

  • Morning day trip to the enchanting town of Sintra.
  • Visit the colorful Pena Palace and the mystical Quinta da Regaleira with its initiation well.
  • Return to Lisbon in the afternoon and take a high-speed train/flight to Seville, Spain.
  • Check into your hotel in Seville and enjoy an evening stroll through the charming Santa Cruz neighborhood.

Day 4: Sunday, May 31, 2026

Location: Seville, Spain

Activities:

  • Morning exploration of Seville's historical marvels: the majestic Seville Cathedral and its Giralda Tower, followed by the stunning Royal Alcázar of Seville.
  • Afternoon spent admiring the grandeur of Plaza de España and relaxing in María Luisa Park.
  • Evening experience a passionate Flamenco show in the vibrant Triana district.

Day 5: Monday, June 1, 2026

Location: Travel to Madrid & City Exploration, Spain

Activities:

  • Morning high-speed train journey from Seville to the capital city, Madrid.
  • Check into your hotel and embark on an afternoon city tour.
  • Visit the bustling Plaza Mayor, the central Puerta del Sol, and the magnificent Royal Palace of Madrid.
  • Evening delight in a traditional tapas crawl in the lively La Latina district.

Day 6: Tuesday, June 2, 2026

Location: Toledo Day Trip from Madrid, Spain

Activities:

  • Full-day excursion to the historic city of Toledo, a UNESCO World Heritage site known as the "City of Three Cultures".
  • Explore the grand Toledo Cathedral, the historic Santa María la Blanca Synagogue, and the beautiful Monasterio de San Juan de los Reyes.
  • Wander through Toledo's narrow medieval streets and enjoy panoramic views from Mirador del Valle.
  • Return to Madrid in the evening for a farewell dinner.

Day 7: Wednesday, June 3, 2026

Location: Departure (Madrid to Kolkata)

Activities:

  • Morning free for last-minute souvenir shopping or a relaxing stroll in Retiro Park, depending on flight schedule.
  • Transfer to Adolfo Suárez Madrid–Barajas Airport (MAD) for your departure back to Kolkata.
  • Cherish the beautiful memories created during your Spain and Portugal adventure!
