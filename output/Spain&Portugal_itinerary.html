7-Day Spain & Portugal Trip

Starting from Kolkata • 28 May 2026 – 3 June 2026

Start
Thu, 28 May 2026
Duration
7 Days / 6 Nights
Route
Kolkata → Barcelona → Madrid → Lisbon → Porto
Style
Culture, food, landmarks, city walks
Day 1 • 28 May

Fly from Kolkata to Barcelona

Kolkata → connecting flight → Barcelona

  • Depart Kolkata and arrive in Barcelona, depending on flight connection.
  • Check in, rest, and take an easy evening stroll around La Rambla or Gothic Quarter.
  • Dinner idea: tapas, paella, and crema catalana.
Day 2 • 29 May

Barcelona: Gaudí & Gothic Quarter

  • Visit Sagrada Família in the morning.
  • Explore Park Güell and Passeig de Gràcia.
  • Evening walk through the Gothic Quarter and Barcelona Cathedral area.
Day 3 • 30 May

Barcelona to Madrid

High-speed train Barcelona → Madrid

  • Morning train to Madrid.
  • Visit the Royal Palace of Madrid and Almudena Cathedral.
  • Evening at Plaza Mayor and Mercado de San Miguel.
Day 4 • 31 May

Madrid: Art, Parks & Food

  • Visit Prado Museum or Reina Sofía Museum.
  • Relax at Retiro Park and see the Crystal Palace.
  • Try churros with chocolate and a tapas crawl in La Latina.
Day 5 • 1 June

Madrid to Lisbon

Short flight Madrid → Lisbon

  • Arrive in Lisbon and check in.
  • Explore Belém Tower, Jerónimos Monastery, and Pastéis de Belém.
  • Sunset from Miradouro da Senhora do Monte.
Day 6 • 2 June

Lisbon: Alfama & Old Town

  • Ride historic Tram 28 early in the day.
  • Explore Alfama, São Jorge Castle, and Praça do Comércio.
  • Evening Fado music dinner experience.
Day 7 • 3 June

Porto Day Trip & Return Journey

Lisbon → Porto by train, then onward return planning

  • Early train to Porto for Ribeira waterfront, Dom Luís I Bridge, and Livraria Lello.
  • Enjoy a Douro riverside lunch.
  • Evening return to Lisbon or connect onward for flight back to Kolkata.

Travel Notes

  • Book long-haul flights and Schengen visa appointments early.
  • Use trains within Spain and Portugal where practical; fly Madrid–Lisbon to save time.
  • Carry light layers: late May and early June are usually pleasant but warm in Iberia.
  • Pre-book Sagrada Família, Park Güell, Prado Museum, and popular train sectors.
